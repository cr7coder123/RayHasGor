MUHAMMED ALI JINNAH-2022 OCTOBER This drawing of Muhammed Ali Jinnah was one of my favorite drawings. I drew Muhmmed Ali Jinnah because he played an important role in separating India and Pakistan and he was a important leader from the past. I used the grid method to draw this because I like to be organized with everything I do. The shading that I did impacted my artwork significantly because before I added the shade it looked plain and 2D.
DODGE CHALLENGER-2023 OCTOBER This drawing started off with me wanting to draw my favorite car the dodge challenger, and it went well and it looked fantastic. But then It looked too plain so I added a bank and the road. I made it look like someone was stealing from the bank by making a hole in the bank. I also made the letter K in bank as if it was falling off to make it look more realistic and dramatic.
